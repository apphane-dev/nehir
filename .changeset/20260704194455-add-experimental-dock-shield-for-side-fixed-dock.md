Add an experimental Dock Shield for side (left/right) fixed-Dock setups.

- New **Dock Shield** (Settings → Diagnostics → Dock Shield; experimental, off by default): masks off-screen columns parked behind a side fixed Dock so no strip leaks into the workspace. Pick its color from presets or a custom color, opt into translucency, or choose a light/dark scheme that follows the system theme.
- Display diagnostics now treat a bottom Dock as expected and flag only a side fixed Dock, offering to enable the Dock Shield.
